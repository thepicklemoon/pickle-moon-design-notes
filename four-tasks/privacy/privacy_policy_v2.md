# Privacy Policy — Four Tasks

**Last updated:** 10 May 2026

This is the privacy policy for **Four Tasks**, a habit-tracking app made by **The Pickle Moon** ([your legal name], ABN [pending]). Contact us at **thepicklemoon@gmail.com** for any questions about this policy or the data we hold.

This policy is written in plain English. We don't think privacy should require a law degree.

---

## What we collect

When you use Four Tasks, we store the following on our servers so the app works:

- A **username** you choose (any text — not your real name unless you decide to use it).
- The **username of your partner** in the app, which you also type in.
- The **four task labels** you set up — whatever you wrote in the boxes.
- The **daily status message** you pick or compose using the in-app message picker.
- **Which days you marked tasks as done**, and which tasks on those days.
- **Which days you marked as rest days.**
- The **coin count** the app keeps for you.
- A few small **settings flags** — your chosen emoji icons, whether you've opted out of the leaderboard, and similar in-app preferences.
- **Bug reports** you submit through the in-app bug catcher, including any text you type.

That's the whole list.

## What we don't collect

We want to be specific about this because it's the more interesting list.

- **No real names, email addresses, phone numbers, or postal addresses.**
- **No IP addresses, device identifiers, or advertising IDs are stored in your record.** (Our hosting provider sees IP addresses at the network level, as any web service does, but we don't keep them or attach them to you.)
- **No location data.**
- **No third-party analytics or tracking** of any kind. We don't use Google Analytics, Facebook SDK, Mixpanel, or anything else like that.
- **No advertising.** The app shows no ads and we don't share data with advertisers.
- **No payment information.** The app is free.

If you don't tell the app something about yourself directly (by typing it in), we don't have it.

## Where your data is stored

Four Tasks stores data on infrastructure provided by **Cloudflare**. Cloudflare's infrastructure is globally distributed, so your data may be stored in any Cloudflare data centre worldwide.

We don't sell, lease, or share your data with any third party. Cloudflare hosts the data on our behalf — they're a hosting provider, not a partner.

## How long we keep your data

For as long as you use the app. If you tap the **reboot button** inside the app and confirm, we delete your record from our servers and you're gone. There is no soft delete and no archived backup — once you reboot, the data is irrecoverable, by design.

If you stop using the app without rebooting, your record stays where it is. We don't proactively delete inactive users at this stage. If you'd like us to delete your record manually, email **thepicklemoon@gmail.com** with the username you used and we'll do it.

## Who can see your data

- **You** — through the app on your device.
- **Your partner in the app** — they can see your task completion and status message because that's the point of a partner app.
- **Us** (The Pickle Moon) — when we look at the database to fix bugs or investigate problems. We don't read individual user data for any other reason.
- **Cloudflare** — at the technical level required to store and serve the data.
- **Nobody else.**

If we add a public leaderboard feature, only your username and coin count are shown to other users, and you can opt out at any time via the in-app long-press on the coin display.

## Children

Four Tasks is not designed for or directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe your child under 13 has used the app and submitted data, email us at **thepicklemoon@gmail.com** and we'll delete the record.

## Your rights

Under Australian privacy law (the Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles), and broadly equivalent laws in other countries:

- **You can ask what we hold about you.** Email us at **thepicklemoon@gmail.com** with your username and we'll send you everything we've got.
- **You can ask us to delete your data.** The reboot button does this instantly. Or email us.
- **You can ask us to correct something.** Most fields are editable inside the app directly. For anything else, email us.
- **You can complain.** If you think we've handled your data badly, you can complain to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at **oaic.gov.au**.

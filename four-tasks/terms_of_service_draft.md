## The deal, plainly

Four Tasks is a habit tracker for one or two people. You pick four
tasks, you do them, the app keeps score. Most of it is free. A
subscription unlocks extras (sticker library access and, when both
partners subscribe, faster coin earning). That's the whole product.

## Your account

There's no email, no password, and no personal profile beyond a display
name, a username, and a chosen sticker. Your identity lives on your
device; if you lose the device, recovery works by re-entering the
values you signed up with (and your partner's, if paired). Keep those
values memorable — we cannot recover an account any other way, because
we don't hold anything else about you.

You must be at least 13 to use Four Tasks (16 where local law requires).

## Pairing

If you pair with a partner, your calendars are visible to each other —
that's the point of the app. Your partner can see your tasks, your
daily messages, and your streak. Un-pairing stops the sharing going
forward but doesn't erase what was already shared while paired. Pair
with someone you trust.

## Subscriptions and payments

Subscriptions are billed through the Apple App Store or Google Play,
not by us directly. Prices are shown in the store before you buy.
Cancellation and refunds follow the store's policies — we can't process
refunds ourselves because we never hold your payment details. Coins
earned in the app have no cash value and can't be exchanged, refunded,
or transferred.

If your subscription lapses, you keep everything you already earned or
bought; you just stop receiving subscriber benefits going forward. Past
days never recalculate.
